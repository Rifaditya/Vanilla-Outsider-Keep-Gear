This mod needs a few graphical assets for the Modrinth page.

## 1. Project Icon

* **Dimensions:** 512x512 pixels (Square).
* **Concept:** A shield or chestplate with a "saving" aura, or a stylized "Keep Inventory" icon.
* **Style:** Pixel art or Minecraft-style render. Matches "Vanilla Outsider" aesthetic.

## 2. Page Banner

* **Dimensions:** ~1200x300 pixels (Wide).
* **Usage:** Goes at the top of `description.md`.
* **Concept:** A player respawning with their armor still on, while scattered items (rotten flesh, dirt) lie on the ground.
* **Text:** "Vanilla Outsider: Keep Gear" (Optional, can be just visual).

## 3. Gallery Images (Optional)

* **Screenshots:**
  * Config Screen showing the "Durability Penalty" and "XP Retention" sliders.
  * Chat message "Your gear has been preserved!" after death.
  * Comparison: Inventory Before Death vs After Death.

## Instructions

1. Upload images to Modrinth gallery or an external host (imgur, discord, etc).
2. Update the `![Banner](...)` link in `description.md` with the direct URL.
